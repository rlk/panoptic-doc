Panoptic

Panoptic lets you interactively browse giga-pixel stereoscopic panoramas and high-resolution planetary surfaces on your laptop display, on your 3D TV, or with your Oculus Rift.

The common thread linking panoramas and planets is their spherical nature. A panorama is a sphere as seen from the inside, and a planet is a sphere as seen from the outside.

Panoptic is open source. Its code resides in the SCM View project, which binds the Spherical Cube Map rendering library with the Thumb virtual reality application framework.

Basic documentation appears in-application. It's enough to get you configured and browsing. The full feature set is documented here.
